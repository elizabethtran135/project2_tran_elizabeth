The Annual Charity Modeling Event

Model4Miracles is a charity modeling event like no other. Combining elements of philanthropy, fashion, pageants, and the overall supermodel experience to raise money for Children's Miracle Network Hospitals of Orlando. Over the last ten years, we have raised over $120,000 for Children's Miracle Network.

Be a Supermodel for a Day!

All ages, sizes, shapes, and heights are welcome! For a $100 donation, models will have their hair and makeup done, a designer outfit to model at the show that day, a model workshop, opportunities to meet agents, scouts, and designers, and amazing photos from the experience.
